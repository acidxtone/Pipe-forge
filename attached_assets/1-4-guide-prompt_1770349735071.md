   - Metric ↔ imperial conversions
   - Basic geometry (area, perimeter)
   - Simple layout calculations

   - Blueprint fundamentals
   - Orthographic drawings
   - Basic piping symbols and abbreviations

   - Dimensions and elevations
   - Exclude: system design, complex calculations

   - Purpose of codes and standards
   - Awareness of CSA and ASME
